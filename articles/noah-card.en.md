# Turning everyone's messages into one card

The Noah message card was a small project created by a group of volunteers. It was not a large technical project, but it felt very natural to me because it was about making something for someone.

## Turning Feelings into Form

We collected messages, designed the layout, printed, and finished the card. It sounds simple, but combining many people's feelings into one object required small adjustments.

## What Stayed with Me

This card project was close to the origin of making things: having something to deliver to someone and using your hands to make it real. Even when the scale is small, a project is strong when the reason for making it is clear.
