# The difficulty of building a model

In the National Satellite Model Contest, we represented the structure and function of a satellite as a physical model and presented the concept. It was not only about making something that looked good. We had to explain why it had that shape and how it would work.

## Understanding by Building

A satellite can feel abstract when you only see it in diagrams. Once I started building, panel placement, deployment mechanisms, internal volume, and center of gravity became real constraints.

- Structure planning
- Functional explanation
- Presentation preparation
- Team role sharing

## What Stayed with Me

Receiving an award was encouraging, but what stayed with me more was the feeling that building deepens understanding. By making the object, knowledge from documents became my own words.
