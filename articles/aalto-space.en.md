# Building a home for space-minded people in Finland

Aalto Space Association is an NPO we started around Aalto University to connect students, researchers, and entrepreneurs interested in space. As Vice-President, I worked on event operations, project management, website development, and community growth.

## Growing the Community

It started as a small Telegram group. From there, we planned events, invited speakers, and shaped an atmosphere that made people want to come back. The community grew from around 50 people to about 600.

- Event planning and operations
- Team task management
- Website development
- Communication in English
- Telegram community operations

## What I Learned

Technology alone does not move a community. You have to know who the space is for, what people take home, and how the organizers build trust over time. Those invisible design choices create the atmosphere.

This experience is close to product building. It is not enough to create features. You also have to design the reason people keep coming back.
