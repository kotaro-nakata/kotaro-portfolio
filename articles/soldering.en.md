"I want to do what you are doing!" That one sentence from my younger brother started this project. His friends joined too, and it became a small event for five kids.<br>
Since they wanted to try soldering, I kept the circuit simple. If everything was soldered correctly, pressing the button made all the LEDs light up, like fireworks going off.<br>
I milled single-sided boards with a PCB machine and prepared enough circuits for everyone. No one got hurt, everyone completed the soldering successfully, and it turned into a really good event.
