# Experiencing satellite systems in a research lab

I joined the Space Systems Engineering Laboratory at Kyushu Institute of Technology as a research assistant. It was a short experience, but it gave me a concrete view of how satellite system research moves through experiments, data organization, and technical meetings.

## What I Saw in the Field

Satellite development does not fit into a single specialty. Electrical systems, mechanical design, communications, thermal behavior, and operations all interact. While supporting research work, I learned the importance of thinking across the whole system.

## How It Connected Later

This experience connected directly to my KOSEN-3 research and later software development. It taught me to look beyond my own assigned task and understand the surrounding constraints and other people's work. Learning that mindset early was valuable.
